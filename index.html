1 – You do this with a pen or pencil. What is it?

A: Write

2 – You do this to your shoe laces. What is it?

A: Tie

3 – The order of a basic affirmative sentence is _________.

A: Subject -verb – object

4 - Adjectives usually come ________.

A: before a noun

5 - Every sentence must have a subject and __________.

A: a verb

6 - What is celebrated on the fourth Thursday of November in the USA?

A: Thanksgiving

7 – What special day is celebrated on the 4th of July in the USA?

A: Independence Day

8 – What is the national bird of the USA?

A: Eagle (bald Eagle)

9 – The Statue of Liberty was a gift from which country?

A: France

10 – What does the idiom “ under the weather “ mean?

A: to be sick or unwell

11 - What does the idiom” let the cat out of the bag”mean?

A: to reveal a secret

12 – What does the idiom “ piece of cake” mean?

A: to be easy to do

13 – Which animals are associated with witches on Halloween?

A: black cats

14 – The most famous British dish is_________.

A: fish and chips

15 – The most famous British drink is___________.

A: tea

16 – What has to be broken before you can use it?

A: an egg

17 – I’m tall when I’m young, and I’m short when I’m old. What am I?

A: a candle

18 – What gets wet while drying?

A: a towel

19 – What is always in front of you but can’t be seen?

A: The Future

20 - I have lakes with no water, mountains with no stone and cities with no buildings. What am I?

A: A map
